Our Commitment to Sustainability

Responsible Logistics for a Better Future

At euroaxislogistics, we believe in the importance of sustainable business practices. We are committed to reducing our environmental impact while delivering exceptional logistics services. Our comprehensive sustainability strategy focuses on four key areas:

Carbon Neutral by 2030

Our ambitious goal to achieve carbon neutrality across all operations through emissions reduction, renewable energy adoption, and carbon offset programs.

Green Fleet Initiative

Transitioning to electric and hybrid vehicles to reduce emissions, with a target of 50% electric fleet by 2025 and 100% by 2030.

Sustainable Packaging

Using recyclable and biodegradable packaging materials, reducing plastic usage by 75%, and implementing circular economy principles in our operations.

Renewable Energy

Powering our facilities with clean, renewable energy sources, with solar panels installed at 80% of our distribution centers and warehouses.
