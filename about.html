Our Story

Building Excellence Since 2005

euroaxislogistics was founded in 2005 in Amsterdam, Netherlands, with a vision to create a logistics company that would set new standards in reliability, efficiency, and customer service across Europe.

What began as a small operation with just five vehicles and a warehouse has grown into one of Europe's leading logistics providers, with operations in 25 countries and a team of over 1,500 dedicated professionals.

Throughout our journey, we have remained committed to our founding principles: delivering excellence in every shipment, embracing innovation, and building lasting relationships with our clients and partners.

Today, euroaxislogistics is at the forefront of the logistics industry, combining cutting-edge technology with deep expertise to provide comprehensive supply chain solutions that help businesses thrive in an increasingly complex global marketplace.
